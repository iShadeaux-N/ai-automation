- Run 5 videos with completely new hook angles (problem/solution framing)

## Posting Checklist (Every Video)

- [ ] Hook lands in first 1-2 seconds
- [ ] Watch time past 8 seconds is earned (cut fluff)
- [ ] TikTok Shop affiliate link updated in bio
- [ ] Caption includes disclosure: `ad | affiliate link in bio ✌️`
- [ ] #TikTokShop and #ad included in hashtags
- [ ] Video logged in pivot_tracker.py after 48 hours
